Welcome to the College of Agriculture

Established in 1995, the College of Agricutlure has been at the forefront of agricultural education and innovation. It started as a small department focused on crop science but quickly grew into a full-fledged college offering various programs in agricultural sciences, engineering, and management.

Over the years, the college faced challenges such as limited funding and the need to modernize facilities. Despite these struggles, it has produced over 5,000 graduates who are leaders in sustainable agriculture, food security, and agri-business. Today, the college is equipped with state-of-the-art research labs and strong industry connections.

  • 1995: Establishment as a department
  • 2000: Upgraded to a College
  • 2010: First international research collaboration
  • 1995: Ranked among the top 10 agricultural colleges in Africa

The College of Agriculture continues to excel with notable achievements like winning national awards for innovation and lauching programs that empower communities through sustainable practices.
