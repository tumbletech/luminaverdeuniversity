Welcome to Lumina Verde University

Where Innovation Meets Sustainability! At LVU, located in the heart of Bamako, Mali, we cultivate minds and nurture dreams. As a leading private institution specializing in agriculture, we proudly offer a wide array of academic programs, from Engineering to Medicine, Law to Education, ensuring every student finds their path to success!

Join us as we pioneer a future rooted in excellence, sustainability, and transformative education. Your journey to brilliance begins here at Lumina Verde University!
