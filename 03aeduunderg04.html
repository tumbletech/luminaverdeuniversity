LVU College of Law

The College of Law at Lumina Verde University (LVU) stands as a beacon of legal education excellence in Mali and the surrounding region. Since its establishment, the college has been dedicated to nurturing a new generation of legal professionals equipped with the knowledge, skills, and ethical grounding to excel in diverse legal legal systems, particularly those influenced by French Civil Law.


History and Legacy

Founded in line with LVU's mission to contribute to Mali's development, the College of Law has consistently produced graduates who play pivotal roles in government, private practice, and international organizations. Drawing from Mali's unique legal heritage and its ties to the French legal tradition, the college integrates local and global perspectives into its teaching, research, and community engagement initiatives.

Legal Clinics

The Legal Clinics at the College of Law, Lumina Verde University, are a cornerstone of the college's committment to experiential learning and community service. These clinics serve as a bridge between theoretical knowledge and practical application, providing students with hands-on experience in the legal field while addressing critical justice needs within Mali.


Achievements
  • Graduates in Leadership: Alumni have held influential positions in the judiciary, public administration, and legal advocacy organizations.
  • Research Excellence: Faculty and students have contributed significantly to research on comparative law, human rights, and legal reform in Mali and Francophone Africa.
  • Community Engagement: Initiatives such as pro bono legal aid and community legal education programs ensure the college remains a vital part of teh community it serves.

Mission

To provide world-class legal education rooted in the traditions of French Civil Law, while fostering critical thinking, ethical integrity, and a commitment to justice in the service of society.


Vision

To be the premier institution for legal education and research in Francophone Africa, recognized for producing innovative, socially responsible, and globally competent legal professionals.

Core Values
  1. Justice and Equity: Upholding fairness in the pursuit of legal education and practice.
  2. Excellence: Commitment to the highest academic and professional standards.
  3. Community Service: Actively engaging with local and international communities to advance legal literacy and access to justice.
