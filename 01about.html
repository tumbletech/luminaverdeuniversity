Meet Mr. Stanley West: Founder of Lumina Verde University

Being a philanthropist from South Carolina, he envisioned a place of higher learning in the heart of Africa. His belief in the transformative power of education led him to establish Lumina Verde University (LVU) in Bamako, Mali.

In March 1992, Stanley embarked on the journey to create an institution that would focus on agriculture, education, and sustainable development, with the goal of addressing food security and improving the livelihoods of the people in Mali and across Africa.

His decision to build such a significant institution in Mali came from his deep desire to provide accessible, world-class education to underserved populations in Sub-Saharan Africa. Stanley's leadership and vision transformed LVU into a beacon of hope, offering not only agricultural programs but a broad range of disciplines, helping to develop professionals in diverse.

Foundation of Lumina Verde University

Establishment Date: March 15, 1992

Construction Completion: September 1992

From the initial groundbreaking ceremony to the completion of the first building, LVU quickly transformed from a vision to reality. The university's rapid development was possible through the generous support of philanthropists like Stanley West and key partnerships with the government of Mali, and international organizations.

The Evolution of LVU

Originally founded as an agricultural school, LVU was a direct response to the need for advanced education in sustainable and food production in Mali. However, thanks to the strong support of the Mali government and a number of international organizations, LVU quickly expanded its reach.

By 1995, LVU had started to offer degrees in other essential fields such as engineering, healthcare, and education. Today, LVU is recognized as one of Africa's leading universities, offering programs that span various sectors and empowering students to drive positive change across the continent.

The Values, Mission, and Vision of LVU

At Lumina Verde University (LVU), we are driven by deep commitment to fostering excellence, innovation, and sustainability. Our core values are rooted int he belief that education is the foundation for transforming lives and building resilient communities.

Our Mission: To provide accessible, high-quality education to students from diverse backgrounds while focusing on practical skills that have real-world applications. We aim to shape leaders who are not only experts in their fields but also responsible citizens committed to sustainable development, ethical practices, and social justice.

Our Vision: To be a leading educational institution in Africa, known for our contributions to agriculture, engineering, healthcare, and other essential sectors. We aspire to create a global network of alumni who will go on to make a tangible difference in the world, from rural farming communities to urban metropolises.

We aim to build a university that continues to inspire, innovate, and lead the way in research, education, and community empowerment for generations to come.
